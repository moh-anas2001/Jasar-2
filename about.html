The first step in the design process is simply calling or e-mailing a brief description of your project needs.
Our first meeting will be at an agreeable time, typically at your home where a discussion of your priorities and design needs can take place, it will be a brainstorming session.

Itâ€™s always helpful to have inspirational pictures available when we meet. During the first meeting photos and measurements will be taken, as well as a discussion of color and style. We will begin to work one on one with you, as well as closely with the architect and contractors as the need arises.

The next step is done back at the studio where selections will be pulled and floor plans drawn beginning to develop the initial idea. Whatever your needs entail to present to you at our next meeting. During this phase appointments may need to be scheduled and selections made with cabinet companies, countertop and flooring, plumbing, electrical fixtures, closet systems and millwork companies. Having over 8 years experience in the interior design industry we have developed a multitude of relationships with several area sub-contractors that we work closely with and recommend.

A mutual creative and interesting solution will be agreed upon and during this process we will continue to answer any questions and fulfill your design needs. Estimates for products chosen will be presented and orders will be placed.

Prior to completion of your project we will follow up and receive product from vendors so we may oversee the installation in a timely manner. The scope of work includes the necessary labor and materials for window treatments, furniture and the last picture hung on the wall and accessory placed.
